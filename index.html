What Is Electrolysis?

Electrolysis is the only FDA-approved method for permanent hair removal. Regardless of the cause—heredity, metabolic, or hormonal conditions—electrolysis will permanently remove unwanted hair.

Treatment involves a professional electrologist inserting a very fine probe (usually thinner than the hair being treated) into the natural opening of the hair follicle, alongside the hair shaft. A tiny amount of electrical current is then applied to destroy the hair growth cells. This method is precise and effective, ensuring the permanent removal of unwanted hair with minimal discomfort.

Preparation & Aftercare

Following these clinical guidelines ensures the most comfortable treatment and the best possible permanent results.

Before Your Session

Hair Management: Please stop plucking and switch to shaving between treatments. A plucked hair can take 5-6 weeks to regrow, and we can only treat hairs visible above the skin. Allow 36-48 hours of growth so hair is long enough to be grasped with tweezers.

Hydration: Drink plenty of water the day before. In the hours directly before your appointment, try to drink 24-48 ounces. Internal moisture is a key factor in the success and comfort of electrolysis.

Comfort: You may take a pain reliever 30 minutes prior. For the upper lip, numbing cream is highly recommended (if not allergic to lidocaine). Ask me for specific recommendations if needed.

  • ✦ Avoid coffee, sodas, or stimulants to keep your nervous system calm.
  • ✦ Discontinue Retin-A, retinoids, or acids (Salicylic/Glycolic) for 1 week prior.
  • ✦ No makeup on the face or deodorant if treating underarms.
  • ✦ Avoid sun exposure 3 days prior and always wear sunscreen.

Post-Treatment Care

The Healing Phase: Redness, swelling, and tiny pinpoint scabbing are normal as the skin barrier recovers. Following these steps for 2 weeks is critical to avoid infection or scarring.

Immediate (24-48 Hours)

Avoid excessive sweating, dirt, hot tubs, or pools. Do not apply makeup or deodorant to the treated area.

Ongoing Recovery

Do not touch, pick, or scratch the area. Use soothing products like Aloe Vera or Bacitracin to promote healing.

  • ✦ Wear loose-fitting clothes (bikini, underarms, nape).
  • ✦ Avoid direct sun or tanning for 48-72 hours.
  • ✦ Do not tweeze or wax the treated area between sessions.
  • ✦ Refrain from Retin-A until all irritation is gone.

What Is Electrolysis?

Electrolysis is the only FDA-approved method for permanent hair removal. Regardless of the cause—heredity, metabolic, or hormonal conditions—electrolysis will permanently remove unwanted hair.

Treatment involves a professional electrologist inserting a very fine probe (usually thinner than the hair being treated) into the natural opening of the hair follicle, alongside the hair shaft. A tiny amount of electrical current is then applied to destroy the hair growth cells. This method is precise and effective, ensuring the permanent removal of unwanted hair with minimal discomfort.

We are committed to providing you with a smooth, comfortable, and effective hair removal experience. From your initial consultation to your final session, here is our path to permanent results.

1. Consultation

Your journey begins with a personalized consultation. We’ll discuss your goals, examine your skin and hair type, and assess your medical history to ensure electrolysis is the right option for you.

2. The Treatment Process

After cleaning the area, a tiny probe is inserted into each hair follicle to destroy the root with a small amount of energy. While you may feel a mild tingling, it is generally well-tolerated, and numbing cream is available for sensitive areas.

2

3. Aftercare

We provide specific instructions to ensure the best results. Any mild redness or swelling usually resolves quickly. We recommend avoiding direct sun exposure and irritating the treated area immediately after.

3

4. Follow-up Sessions

To achieve permanent removal, multiple sessions are required to catch hair in the correct growth cycle. We’ll schedule follow-ups based on your personalized plan to bring you closer to smooth, hair-free skin.
